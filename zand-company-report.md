- Corporate/institutional digital banking (live since 30 Jun 2022). Retail was planned for late 2022 at licence time; current site markets corporate/institutional/fintech/wealth only — whether retail launched or was shelved is UNVERIFIED. — https://www.zand.ae/en/about-us
- Institutional digital-asset custody (VARA-approved, live Dec 2024): private keys in onshore UAE HSMs; corporate/institutional only. — https://www.zand.ae/en/news/zand-revolutionizes-uae-banking-with-institutional-grade-digital-asset-custody
